Meaning of this Portfolio

My portfolio is a tool to track my progress toward both general and individual learning goals. It captures new skills, knowledge, and challenges I have overcome and makes growth visible even when it is not reflected in code or artifacts.

By breaking general goals into small milestones I can celebrate quick wins, stay motivated, and learn from the process. Over time it becomes a library of my competencies that supports internship and job applications. .
